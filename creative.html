Our Approach

Not the Typical Agency Model

SENA Advertising was built around broadcast production excellence. Our creative team has collectively written, directed, and produced more than 10,000 TV and radio commercials — and the body of work speaks for itself.

We don't just make commercials. We understand client objectives, stay deeply involved from account service through final edit, and apply the same level of attention and craft to every project regardless of budget size.

Whether you're launching a product, driving retail traffic, or building long-term brand equity, we approach every brief with the strategic rigor and creative ambition it deserves.
